Notes

Password data will not be stored on a server and is only processed in the browser.

Strong passwords should be long and complex. Use the entire keyboard, incorporating numbers, symbols (~!@#$%^&*()-_+={}[]|;:<>,./?), and both lowercase and uppercase letters. The longer, the better. A minimum of eight characters is recommended. Do not use personal information like a dog's name or graduation year. Do not make your password identical to your username or email.
